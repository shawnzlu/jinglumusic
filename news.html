Welcome to this website about excellent Hip Hop and R&B Singer Jing Lu, home of the popular singing sensation in the Chinese-Canadian community and overseas. Jing Lu is a multi-award winning singer who is currently working on her first debut EP. The highly anticipated EP will be a combination of many musical styles such as hip-hop, pop, rock and funk that will refresh the ear of listener. All the songs are written by Jing Lu herself and produced by Cory Noziglia from 199 Studios. The album will be released hopefully sometime in the summer, so please check back for any further updates. To get a sneak peek of music of Jing Lu, please visit the video section and audio section for some of her finished productions.

November, 2006

Congratulation to Jing Lu for winning the Second-runner up in the First Minnan Singing Award in Fujian China. Jing Lu recently traveled all the way to Fujian China to represent Canada to compete in the First Minnan Singing Award. After 3 weeks of furious competition over 50 representatives from 4 countries, Jing Lu proudly took home the second-runner up.

Happily connected, the first self written Mandarin song of Jing Lu is being aired on Toronto First Radio.
