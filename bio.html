Jing Lu emerged as a new singing sensation when she won the 2001 New Talent Singing Award Toronto at the age of 15. In October 2001, Jing represented Toronto to compete in the International New Talent Singing Contest in Guangzhou, China. As one of the youngest contestant in the contest, she achieved that second-runner up award and became the first Toronto contestant to win an award in this International singing event. Furthermore, she is also the winner of Grand Cathay Singing Competition and 2nd runner up of International Minnan Singing/Song Writing Contest. Most recently, Jing traveled once again to China to compete in International Minnan Singing Award. This competition involved 50 contestants from 4 countries. Through a series of elimination, Jing proceeded to the finals and brought home the second runner up prize.

Jing Lu gains popularity in the Chinese-Canadian community through her remarkable performances and a constant appearance in major events and showcases such as the Chinese Beauty Pageant, the C.N.E and New Years Countdown etc. She is a regular guest performance for local media such as Fairchild TV, Fairchild Radio, Toronto First Radio and Phoenix TV. As an active participant in local Chinese entertainment productions; she performed many times for the casinos concerts, Charity events and Cultural celebrations. In June 2006, Jing Lu had the pleasure of guest performing in the Launching of 2008 Beijing Olympic Theme Song concert at the Fallsview Casino Niagara as the only local artist. Beyond local showcases, she has been performed for functions that took place in the United States. She appeared twice in Chicago twice as a special guest in two major Chinese Singing Events where the feedback has been tremendous.

This multi-award winner Jing Lu is currently majoring in vocal music at York University, as well as taking dance lessons and private vocal coaching. Her ability to sing and dance has helped her to become an all-around artist. In 2006, Jing began to explore the creative side of her musical talent, as a song writer. One of her collaborated composition has won the gold award in the Minnan Song Writing contest and was voted to be in the audience's top 10 favorite songs. Another one of her compositions Happily Connected has been aired on Toronto First Radio and it hit the radio's music chart and ranked number one for two consecutive weeks. This song became the theme song for a major karaoke contest; and it is continuously being aired daily. During a recent singing contest in China, her newly finished production Let's Party Now was featured in the competition and was very well received by the Chinese audience. This song will be performed at additional events in the near future. Currently, she has written a great numbers of original compositions, and is in the process of completing her first album towards her desire to grow as an independent songwriter and performer.
